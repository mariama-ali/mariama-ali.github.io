01 / E-Commerce Library Project

Developed a E-Commerce system for my personal book collection. The interface allows you to browse and explore books including a recommendation section which filters through data and selects similar books. You can also sort by prices low to high , high to low, and rating. As well as enabling visitors to purchase books by adding them to a shopping cart and check out using a variety of online payment options.

Built with : HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js
